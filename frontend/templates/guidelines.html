Safety & Guidelines

Resources and best practices for identifying and reporting cyber threats.

Before You Report
  • Collect screenshots, email headers, and timestamps.
  • Do NOT share passwords or bank PINs in the form.
  • Preserve the original suspicious communication.
Protection Tips
  • Always hover over links to inspect the actual URL.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all accounts.
  • Verify sender identity before sharing any sensitive data.

Connect with national authorities and support centers for urgent cybercrime response.
